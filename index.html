My favourites

Mountains

Mountains are not only majestic giants that touch the sky, but also humbling teachers that remind us of the vastness and strength within ourselves.

Cloud

Clouds are the ethereal brushstrokes of the sky, painting an ever-changing masterpiece that dances with the wind and ignites the imagination.

Ocean

The ocean is a boundless tapestry of secrets and stories, where the rhythm of waves whispers ancient tales and the depths hold mysteries yet to be explored.

Movie

Movies are portals that transport us to extraordinary realms, stirring our emotions, inspiring our dreams, and reminding us of the limitless power of storytelling.

Sorrow

Sorrow, though heavy and relentless, carries within it the potential for profound growth and resilience, as it is through our deepest pains that we discover the strength of our spirit and the capacity for healing.
