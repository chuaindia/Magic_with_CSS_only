My favourites

Mountains

Mountains are not just geographical features; they are realms of grandeur, where the earth meets the sky and the human spirit finds solace in their majestic presence.

Cloud

Clouds are the ethereal brushstrokes of the sky, painting an ever-changing masterpiece that dances with the wind and ignites the imagination.

Ocean

The ocean is a boundless tapestry of secrets and stories, where the rhythm of waves whispers ancient tales and the depths hold mysteries yet to be explored.

Movie

Movies are not just a form of entertainment; they are a powerful medium that can transport us to different worlds, ignite our imaginations, evoke a range of emotions, and leave an indelible mark on our souls.

Sorrow

Sorrow, like a heavy cloud, casts its shadow upon the heart, but within its depths lie the seeds of resilience and the potential for growth.

Trekking

Trekking is a journey of the body, mind, and spirit, where every step taken reveals the beauty of the world and the strength within oneself.

Love

Love is a force that transcends time and space, capable of healing wounds, igniting souls, and creating a symphony of emotions that reverberates through every corner of our being.

Temple

Temples stand as timeless witnesses, embodying the sacred connection between the divine and humanity, inviting us to pause, peace, reflect, and find solace in the presence of something greater than ourselves.
